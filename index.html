What do Polar Bears Eat

The diet of a polar bear is very diverse. The known favorite food of polar bears are seals, however if a polar bear is starving it will resort to other food sources.

Foods that these animals usually eat consist of:

  • baby seals
  • carrions
  • sea birds
  • eggs

Foods that that these polar bears will resort to for food as well consist of:

  1. Earless seals
  2. Narwhal
  3. Bowhead whale
  4. Beluga Whale
  5. Walrus
